Privacy Policy

Travel Checklist is a personal productivity app. It runs as a Progressive Web App (PWA) and a Trusted Web Activity (TWA) on Android.

Last updated: September 05, 2025

What data we collect

We do not collect personal data. Your checklist content is stored locally on your device using browser technologies such as LocalStorage and the Origin Private File System (OPFS). It is not transmitted to our servers.

How your data is used

  • To save your lists in your browser or on-device storage.
  • To export/import your lists when you choose to do so.
  • To print a copy when you choose to do so.

Third-party services

Travel Checklist does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash reporting. If we add any third-party service in the future, we will update this policy and the app will disclose it.

Permissions

The app may ask for permission to read or write files you explicitly select (e.g., when importing or exporting your checklist). No other device permissions are requested.

Children’s privacy

Travel Checklist is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will address it.

Your choices

  • You can clear your data by using the app’s reset function or by clearing your browser/app storage.
  • You can export or delete the JSON file you saved to your device at any time.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact support@appintelligence.com.
